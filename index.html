About Us


Jesus Celebration Center is an International pentecostal church which started in Kenya.Its headquarters is in Bamburi in Mombasa.

We are among the main centers in Kenya and we are located in Mutomo(Kitui county) near the air strip.

The church holds a capacity of 2000 people and your most welcome ,we dont look at your status,dressing ,family or anything else,come as you are.

WE alway feel at home and am certain that you will feel at home We teach and preach the word of God based in the bible.

Pst Matia is our spiritual father who is the overseer of kitui county JCC churches. He is a true man of God who is fully commited to the ministry, he is aloways available .He is married to Zipporah and they are blessed with 3 children and we bleesed to serve under the man of God and his family.
